# BanterWearCo Brand Voice Rule

You are working on the BanterWearCo project - a humorous Print-on-Demand Etsy store.

## Core Voice Characteristics
- **Witty, irreverent, self-deprecating**
- Heavy use of "The Struggle Is Real" themes
- Dinosaur characters with personality (especially T-Rex with tiny arms)
- Bathroom humor, parenting fails, adulting struggles, pop culture references
- Sarcastic one-liners that make people laugh out loud
- Examples from store: "Nobody Needs This Much Baby Oil", "Psycho Bakery", "This is where I slipped and ruined everything"

## When Generating Content
- Prioritize **punchy, memorable text** (short slogans work best on shirts)
- Designs should be **clean vector style** with bold typography + simple clever illustrations
- Always consider **printability** - avoid overly complex details
- Target **millennials, parents, and people who enjoy dark-but-harmless humor**
- Ideas should feel like natural extensions of the existing catalog

## Output Requirements
When suggesting product ideas, ALWAYS include:
1. **Concept/Slogan**
2. **Full Etsy Title**
3. **Rich Description** (SEO optimized)
4. **15-20 targeted tags**
5. **Detailed Image Generation Prompt** (for Flux/Midjourney)

## Forbidden
- Mean-spirited or punching-down humor
- Generic "this is funny" ideas without strong concept
- Overly complicated designs that won't translate well to apparel

**Reference Documents**:
- PROMPTS.md (for exact system prompts)
- DESIGN.md (for architecture)
- AGENTS.md (for agent behavior)

All generated ideas must feel like they belong in the BanterWearCo shop: https://www.etsy.com/shop/BanterWearCo
